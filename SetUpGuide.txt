**--ALL SCRIPTS PROVIDED ARE PROVIDED IN THE MODEL DynamicFootsteps2ByDebian.rbxm--**
-follow the steps I provided and it should be fine

    Put the "FootstepSounds" folder in ReplicatedStorage
    Put the "FootStepServer" inside ServerScriptService
    Put "DynamicFootsteps" inside StarterPlayer > StarterPlayerScripts

**--ADDING MORE FOOTSTEP SOUNDS--**
    In the FootstepSounds folder inside your ReplicatedStorage,
    duplicate/insert a new Sound and rename it according to the Material
    you want to have a dynamic footstep sound

    ! Make sure to match the Material name (Plastic/Rock/Dirt/Grass etc)

**--FOOTSTEP SOUNDS DOESNT MATCH THE CHARACTER'S FOOTSTEP?--**
    In your DynamicFootsteps inside the StarterPlayerScripts
    change the value of these codes
    local STEP_INTERVAL_WALK = 0.25
    local STEP_INTERVAL_RUN = 0.24
    - reduce it to make the Footstep sounds faster
    - increase the value to make the footstep sounds slower
